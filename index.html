📏 Rating Criteria

🎤 Overall Presentation

Evaluates the quality of slides, verbal delivery, clarity of communication, confidence, and the logical flow of the presentation.

📋 Content

Evaluates depth of research, accuracy of information, relevance to the case, and quality of insights or recommendations provided.

🤝 Group Participation

Evaluates equal contribution from all members, teamwork, ability to handle Q&A as a group, and overall collaboration quality.
